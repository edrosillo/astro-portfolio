# Neon Cyber Poster

This project was created using Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. The goal was to capture the essence of 80s sci-fi aesthetics.

## Process

1.  Sketched the layout on paper.
2.  Created vector shapes in Illustrator.
3.  Added texture and lighting effects in Photoshop.
